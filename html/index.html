What is Zyme?

Zyme is a unique Nasal Spray for the whole family, Zyme contains Lysozyme, Which is one of the most powerful natural antibacterial available naturally in a number of human secretions like Mucus, Saliva and Tears. Lysozyme attacks Peptidoglycans found in the cell wall of bacteria, Especially Gram positive bacteria. At the same time Zyme contains Cellulose and Isotonic saline(0.9% NaCl) which frees and clean nose from nasal mucus and helps to fluidifies nasal secretions and makes breathing easier in patients suffering from Runny nose.

How to use?

Insert the nozzle in to each nostril and spray one or more puffs in to each nostril. After use, Please put the protection Cap back on the nozzle, Repeat this process according to the need. Zyme Nasal Spray contains a Safe and Natural ingredients could be suitable for long time use if necessary with Babies, Children and Adults after Doctor or Pharmacist recommendation.

Who can use Zyme?

Any one suffering from Rhinitis and after E.N.T Surgical procedure as a cleansing and Anti-bacterial agent.


Zyme Safety Profile:
Zyme considered as a safe Nasal Spray for all age groups according to US & European Regulations.

How do I store zyme?

Please store the product under 25 °C and away from direct sunlight.

What are Precautions I have to consider?

Do not use in case of known allergy to one of product components. In case of Hypersensitivity please stop using the product and contact your Doctor. Keep out of reach of children under 3 years. Do not use after the Expiry date shown on outer box.
